# Privacy Policy - TEOS Wallet

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025  
**Last Updated:** January 1, 2025

## 1. Introduction

Elmahrosa International E.S.T. ("we," "our," or "us") operates TEOS Wallet to provide access to the TEOS Network ecosystem. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Pi Network Information
- **Pi Username**: Collected during 1 Pi payment verification
- **Pi Payment ID**: Transaction identifier for wallet unlock
- **Pi Network Address**: Generated after successful verification

### 2.2 Blockchain Addresses
- **Solana Wallet Address**: Automatically generated upon Pioneer verification
- **TEOS Network Address**: Your unique identifier in the ecosystem
- **Transaction History**: Public blockchain data from your address

### 2.3 Civic Verification Data
- **Petition Confirmation**: Boolean tracking of Change.org petition signature
- **Telegram Join**: Verification of @elmahrosapi membership
- **Facebook Follow**: Confirmation of TEOS Egypt page follow (@Teosegypt)
- **Twitter Follow**: Verification of @KING_TEOS follow

### 2.4 Contribution Information
- **SOL Contribution Amount**: Value sent to campaign wallet
- **Badge Tier**: Bronze, Silver, Gold, or Sovereign status
- **TEOS Token Allocation**: Reward distribution records
- **Staking Activity**: APY calculations and reward history

### 2.5 Technical Data
- **IP Address**: For security and fraud prevention
- **Device Information**: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
- **Session Data**: Login timestamps, feature usage patterns
- **Error Logs**: For debugging and service improvement

### 2.6 Voluntary Information
- **Email Address**: If provided for notifications (optional)
- **Social Media Profiles**: Links to verified accounts (optional)
- **Referral Data**: If you participate in the referral program

## 3. How We Use Your Information

### 3.1 Wallet Functionality
- Generate and manage your Solana and Pi Network addresses
- Process token transactions (send, receive, swap)
- Calculate and distribute mining rewards
- Enable Nilex DEX integration

### 3.2 Verification & Access Control
- Verify Pioneer status and civic completion
- Enforce badge tier requirements for features
- Prevent duplicate accounts and fraud
- Manage contributor reward distribution

### 3.3 Communication
- Send transaction confirmations
- Notify about verification status changes
- Announce new features and ecosystem updates
- Provide customer support

### 3.4 Analytics & Improvement
- Understand wallet usage patterns
- Optimize user experience and interface
- Identify and fix bugs
- Plan new features based on demand

### 3.5 Compliance & Security
- Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
- Comply with Egyptian and international regulations
- Respond to legal requests from authorities
- Protect against unauthorized access

## 4. Information Storage & Security

### 4.1 Local Storage
- **Verification Status**: Stored in browser localStorage
- **Wallet Preferences**: Settings and UI customizations
- **Transaction Cache**: Recent activity for faster loading

We do NOT store your private keys or recovery phrases. You are solely responsible for safeguarding these.

### 4.2 Server Storage
- **Pioneer Database**: Usernames, addresses, civic status, contribution amounts
- **Transaction Logs**: Blockchain interaction history
- **Analytics Data**: Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics

### 4.3 Security Measures
- **Encryption**: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
- **Access Controls**: Limited team access to production systems
- **Regular Audits**: Security reviews of smart contracts and infrastructure
- **Backup Systems**: Redundant data storage for disaster recovery

### 4.4 Data Retention
- **Active Pioneers**: Data retained indefinitely while wallet is in use
- **Inactive Accounts**: Anonymized after 24 months of inactivity
- **Transaction History**: Public blockchain data (permanent, immutable)
- **Support Tickets**: Deleted after resolution + 12 months

- **Verification State**: Remember your Pioneer status
- **Preferences**: UI settings, language selection

### 6.2 Optional Cookies

## 7. Your Rights

### 7.1 Access & Portability
- Request a copy of your stored data (email info@elmahrosa-expo.com)
- Export transaction history from the wallet interface
- View blockchain data using public explorers (Solscan, DexLab)

### 7.2 Correction
- Update civic verification status in the wallet settings
- Correct errors in contact information
- Request manual review if verification failed incorrectly

### 7.3 Deletion
- Delete your TEOS Wallet account (irreversible)
- We will remove personal data from our servers
- **Note**: Blockchain data (addresses, transactions) is permanent

### 7.4 Objection & Restriction
- Opt out of marketing communications
- Request restriction of data processing (may limit features)
- Object to analytics tracking

### 7.5 Withdraw Consent
You may revoke consent for data processing by discontinuing use of TEOS Wallet.

## 8. Children's Privacy

TEOS Wallet is not intended for users under 18 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover underage users, we will delete their accounts.

## 9. International Data Transfers

TEOS Network is based in Egypt. If you access the wallet from outside Egypt, your data may be transferred internationally. We comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 10. Blockchain Transparency

### 10.1 Public Data
- All Solana transactions are publicly visible
- Wallet addresses can be linked to your identity
- Token holdings and balances are transparent

### 10.2 Privacy Best Practices
- Use separate addresses for different purposes
- Avoid linking wallet addresses to social media publicly
- Be cautious about sharing transaction history
